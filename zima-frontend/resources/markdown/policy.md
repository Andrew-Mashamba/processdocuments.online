# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 2026**

## Introduction

Process Documents Online ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our document processing service.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Email address and name when you create an account
- **Uploaded Files**: Documents you upload for processing (PDF, Excel, Word, PowerPoint, images)
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with our tools and services

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, device type
- **Log Data**: IP address, access times, pages viewed
- **Cookies**: Session cookies for functionality and preferences

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide document processing services
- Improve our tools and user experience
- Send service-related communications
- Ensure security and prevent fraud
- Comply with legal obligations

## File Processing and Storage

### Document Handling
- Files are processed on secure servers
- Uploaded files are automatically deleted after 24 hours
- We do not access, read, or analyze the content of your documents
- Files are encrypted during transmission (SSL/TLS)

### Quick Tools (No AI)
- Files processed through Quick Tools are handled locally where possible
- No permanent storage of processed files
- Immediate deletion after download

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:
- **Service Providers**: Hosting and infrastructure partners
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or legal process
- **Business Transfers**: In case of merger or acquisition

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your account and data
- Export your data
- Opt-out of marketing communications

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- SSL/TLS encryption
- Secure server infrastructure
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and monitoring

## Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## International Users

Our servers are located in secure data centers. By using our service, you consent to the transfer of your data to these locations.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or service notification.
